The professional & secure luggage delivery service

Operating between London and Heathrow Airport, AirPortr™ provides a convenient and flexible luggage delivery service for your highly valuable business or leisure customer.

Provide an additional service proposition to your customers whilst generating incremental commission based revenue. Whether its an early business meeting on arrival in London or visiting tourist attractions after a morning checkout from the hotel, AirPortr™ allows your customers to make the most of their valuable time, stress free.

  • Airport to hotel
  • Hotel to airport

How it works

  • Step 1 Booking the AirPortr™ service.

    Your customers can either book direct via our website, seamlessly through your e-commerce site or by you booking on their behalf via our agent portal. You charge the customer and we'll bill you, less your commission, at the end of the month!

  • Step 2 Customers leave their bag with our Portr's™

    On arrival at Heathrow airport, your customers leave their luggage at our conveniently located reception desks. After their bag is scanned and security checked, it bag is stored securely on airport as they continue their journey stress and bag free.

  • Step 3 We deliver their luggage to their hotel.

    AirPortr's™ logistics partner, City Sprint will take their luggage from the airport to their hotel. Airportr™ will keep the customer and hotel informed every step of the way from collection through to delivery. Customers can even track their bag's real-time GPS location through our website!

  • Step 4 Customers pick their luggage up and check-in.

    Having made the most of their transit day, your customer arrives at the hotel, to check-in knowing their luggage is waiting for them either at the reception desk or up in their rooms.

  • Step 3 Luggage Collection and delivery

    Bags will be collected within 4 hours of the customers' checkout and delivered securely to the Airportr™ desk at the specified departure terminal. We keep the customer and hotel informed every step of the delivery process via email and text notifications. GPS real-time tracking is also available via our website.

  • Step 4 Customer luggage collection.

    Later that day or evening, with their booking reference and approved ID, the customer can collect their luggage from our desk where the bag has been stored and security checked. They proceed to departures to check-in for their flight!

Simple, structured pricing

  • Incl. £10,000 loss or damage cover

Commission

Up to 150 bookings 10% commission is earned on net revenue booked
From 151 to 300 bookings 12% commission is earned on net revenue booked
Over 301 bookings 15% commission is earned on net revenue booked

Commissions are based on calendar month booking volumes and are earned on revenues net of VAT. At the start of each month the volume of bookings made are reset back to zero.
Commissions are paid in arrears on a calendar month basis 30 days after the previous month ends.

5: How can I make a booking?

You can either make a booking using our web based agent portal www.portr.com/agent or we can integrate into your e-commerce site.

6: How much does the customer pay?

We have kept this nice and simple. The customer does not need to worry about figuring out their bags weight and dimensions. It's one price for all sizes. They just need to tell us whether its ‘hand' or ‘hold' luggage so we know roughly how much space our vehicles will have. We have broken London up into 3 Zones (see zone map), so depending on which zone you fall into their first bag will cost either £14.99, £24.99 or £34.99. Every bag thereafter is £5 per bag, any size any number.*
* subject to fair usage Policy

7: When will their luggage be delivered?

Our goal is to deliver their luggage within 4 hours of it being dropped off either at the airport or hotel reception. We can't always guarantee that their bags will be delivered within this timescale as we all know the inner-City roads can get busy. However, with our fleet running advance traffic monitoring and route planning technology we expect any deviation from this goal to be minimal. When using the Hotel to Airport service, we know that having the customer luggage at the airport well in advance of checking-in is important, so please bear this in mind when specifying their collection time (which should coincide with your airline's check-in requirements).

8: Can I track where the customer's bag is?

Our website and mobile site will be packed full of great features to help you get the most out of your booking and the AirPortr™ experience, however one of our favourites has to be the real time GPS tracking of their bag. If you want absolute peace of mind, you can log into your account and watch it every step of the way on our user friendly map. Otherwise, you can choose your communication preferences in account settings, and we can keep you updated with their bag's process by text and or email.

9: Can I book on the day of travel?

At Airportr™ we aim to be as flexible as possible, which means being able to book on the day of travel, However, to ensure that we can always provide the service at the time that you need and to reduce waiting time at desks, we encourage you to pre-book the customer's journey in advance. We wouldn't want to disappoint!

10: Can I book multiple bags and journeys?

You can book multiple bags under one booking. You will also be able to add a return journey to the booking. However, in the event that you would like to add another customer (or different date, number of bags and destination), you will need to complete a new booking.

14: Can I come and work at AirPortr™?

15: How do I know that customer bags will be stored and transported safely?

At Airportr™ we take security very (...very) seriously. Any luggage we handle is security checked in the airport and held in a secure CCTV monitored area before being collected. Our qualified portrs™ have been trained in careful and priority handling of luggage, and currently service some of the industries leading airlines. Our portrs™ are subject to strict interview processes and CRB checks. Our vehicles are locked at all times when in service, no matter how quick a drop is. Customer bags are security tagged when in our possession making them incredibly hard to misplace and in the unlikely event that they are lost, they are easier to recover! We know this all sounds very serious ...it's because we care about our responsibility to our customers.

16: What happens if something gets damaged or lost?

In the unlikely and unfortunate event that something gets damaged or lost (either your luggage or contents) when in our care, simply report the damaged item to one of our team. As part of the Airportr™ service the customer gets inclusive cover of up to £10,000 per piece of luggage, so on acceptance of the claim, they will be reimbursed for the value of the damaged item. We will also give the customer their next Airportr™ journey for free to say sorry! Please refer to our Terms and Conditions section of our website for further details on how to claim and qualifications.

17: When or how do I get charged for the service?

If the customer books direct through your own e-commerce site then we will invoice you less any commission earned. The customer will be charged through your site.

If you book through our agent portal then again we will invoice you less any commission earned. However you must remember to add the charge to the customer's bill that you issue to them.

If the customer books directly on the Airportr™ website then you do not need to do anything.

18: What happens if the customer's travel arrangements change?

It's not a problem. Airportr™ is designed to be flexible and to cater for their travel needs. So unlike many others out there we won't charge you any fees for advance booking amendments or cancellations – even when made on the day. So long as one of our vehicles hasn't started its journey to collect the customer's luggage, changes to their booking won't cost you a penny. What's more, if their travel plans change because their flight is delayed or cancelled, we will update their booking accordingly for free. For more details see our Terms & Conditions.

19: What are the benefits of being a registered partner?

You can earn incremental revenue, increase your brand awareness and make it easier for customers to use the Airportr™ service thus providing them with a reason to recommend your service.
